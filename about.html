Who We Are

Certified Civil Engineering & Construction Excellence

Emeka Chizel Enterprise Limited is a fully registered company incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020, with CAC Registration Number RC 7382565. Established on March 5th, 2024, and registered in Abuja, Nigeria, we are proudly based in Enugu, serving clients across the nation.

As a certified civil engineer and registered professional, we deliver trusted, professional solutions across three major sectors with an unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
